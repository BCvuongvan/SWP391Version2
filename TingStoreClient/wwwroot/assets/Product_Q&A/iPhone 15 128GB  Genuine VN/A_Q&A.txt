Question: What is upgraded iPhone 15 camera?
Answer: The iPhone 15 phone is still only equipped with dual cameras, but Apple has upgraded the main lens to 48MP resolution. Along with that, the phone supports taking impressive 2x zoom photos.

Question: What's upgraded iPhone 15 configuration
Answer: iPhone 15 is focused by Apple on upgrading the screen design, color and camera. In terms of hardware, although not upgraded too much, the device still owns the new Apple A16 chip along with the optimal iOS operating system.
